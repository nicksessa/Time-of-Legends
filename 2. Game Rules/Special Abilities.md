# Special Abilities

|dice: 1d12|Ability|
|:-:|---|
|1|**Charismatic** - One free Bowman or Man-at-Arms after each victorious battle.|
|2|**Item: Cloak of the Dragon** - This cloak mysteriously confers the ability for the wearer to be immune to non-magical missile fire.|
|3|**Tough as Nails** - Gain +1 to Toughness|
|4|**Lionheart** - Reroll failed morale checks.|
|5|**Decisive** - Your side can always choose to go first.|
|6|**I Have The High Ground!** - You can choose which side of the battlefield to deploy on.|
|7|**Purity** - This character and all allied figures within short range have +1 DR vs demons.|
|8|**Lady Patron** - On a roll of an 11 or 12 on the Heroes Advancement Table will result in a Lady.|
|9|**Huntsman** - Once per battle, this character can move through wooded terrain at no movement penalty.|
|10|**Seafarer** - Once per battle, this character can move through water terrain at no movement penalty.|
|11|**Hunting Dog** [^1] - This character has a rather large and vicious hunting dog that can be commanded to attack foes.|
|12|**Falconer** [^1] - This character has an trained falcon that can be commanded to attack foes.|
^specialAbilitiesTable

[1] If lost or killed, the character will automatically find a new dog or falcon after 2d6 days.

## Lionheart

## Decisive
Your side can always choose to go first.

## Purity
This character and all allied figures within short range have +1 DR vs demons.

## Seafarer
Once per battle, this character can move through water terrain at no movement penalty.

foes.
