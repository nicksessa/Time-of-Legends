# Injury Chart

When figures are taken out of action during a skirmish, roll on the following tables depending

The Mordheim permanent injury chart, **used to determine the long-term effects of severe wounds sustained during battles**, is typically resolved using a D66 roll (two six-sided dice, one representing tens and the other units). Results from this roll determine the nature of the permanent injury or outcome for a warrior.

Some injuries are explicitly permanent and cannot be treated, including Chest Wound, Blinded in One Eye, and Old Battle Wound, as noted in optional rules. Other injuries, such as Leg Wound, Smashed Leg, and Hand Injury, may be addressed using the Limb Surgery table, while Madness and Nervous Condition can be treated via the Brain Surgery table.
