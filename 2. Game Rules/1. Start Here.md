## Starting Home Castle

Roll on the [[Random Deployment]] table to determine your starting location and Lord.

`dice: [[Random Deployment#^deploymentCharacters]]`

King [[List of Kings#Arthur\|Arthur]] cannot be your starting Lord.  If your starting location lists him as your Lord, roll on the random Knight chart and that knight will be your Starting Lord and his castle your Home Castle.
