| 9   | Sack of Caltrops: A sack filled with spiked traps designed to impede the movement of horsemen and light infantry. A figure may spend their turn scattering the traps in a Short Range circle from the center of their base. The traps inflict 1 STR 3 wound on any figure passing through the circle. One use only. |
| 10   | Keg of Black Powder: A keg of explosive powder. A figure may place the keg as their action, and ignite it with a successful Initiative test. After one full turn, the keg detonates. Each figure within a Short Range suffers 1 STR 5 wound. One use only. |
