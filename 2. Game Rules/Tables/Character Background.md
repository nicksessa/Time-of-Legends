## Contacts

Roll 1d12 three times on this table to determine what prominent characters are associated with your band.

| dice: 1d12 | Result  |
|:---:|---|
| 1 | Soldier from the same household: Another soldier in your retinue.  |
| 2 | Soldier from rival household: A soldier in the service of a rival lord.  |
| 3 | Mercenary: A hired sword in your lord’s service.  |
| 4 | Household Knight: A knight in your lord’s retinue.  |
| 5 | Local Peasant:  A sympathetic local civilian  |
| 6 | Outlaw: A local criminal, exiled onto the fringes of society.  |
| 7 | Merchant: A roving trader.  |
| 8 | Religious figure: A priest from the local area or part of the warband’s camp followers.  |
| 9 | Minor Noble: A count, baron or similarly titled figure  |
| 10 | Refugee: A dispossessed civilian, fleeing the horrors of war.  |
| 11 | Straggler: A soldier left behind in the wake of a fast moving army  |
| 12 | Camp Follower: Whores, tradesmen, family members and crooks trailing along behind the army. |
^contacts
