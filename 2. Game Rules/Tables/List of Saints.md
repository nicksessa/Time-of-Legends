# Saints

## Divine Favor
For every additional Divine Favor (DF) point spent over the listed cost, the Chance of Success increases by one.  For example, if the DF Cost is 5, but you spend 6 points, you gain a +1 to the Chance.

## Virtue Points
For every Virtue Point over the minimum, the DF Cost is reduced by a like amount to a minimum of 1. For example, if your Virtue Level is 5 and the Minimum Virtue required is 2, then you reduce the DF cost by 3 points to a minimum of 1.

## Attribute Bonuses
All attribute bonuses are temporary and last 1 encounter/dungeon unless specified.

## Maximum Divine Favor

The maximum number of Divine Favor Points is 10 and the minimum is 0.  These limits can be modified by items or other circumstances.

## Regaining Divine Favor
Divine Favor can be regained by resting, spending time in prayer at a church or completing a pilgrimage.

# Bonus to Combat Stats
Combat stats are: Melee ATT, Melee DEF, Ranged

# Reputation
Any bonus to Reputation gives a bonus on Castle reaction rolls.
